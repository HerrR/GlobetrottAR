  • How it works

    Using GlobetrottAR with your Android phone, aim your camera onto the globe to see an augmented explorer travel the earth along with facts about the travels, all controlled by a scrollable timeline.

  • Project goals

    We wanted to create an exploratory experience using augmented reality technologies in tandem with a tangible and classic object - an actual world globe.

  • Technologies used

    We used Unity together with Vuforia to track the globe, along with Maya and Blender to create the 3D models.

  • Challenges

    Tracking the globe consistently has been the biggest challenge, as it does not have clear edges for Vuforia to track. As Vuforia isn't open source, that also made it harder for us to tailor the experience.

  • Lessons learned

    Spherical image tracking is hard, especially if you use a locked framework with limited to no possibility of customizing the tracking behaviour.
